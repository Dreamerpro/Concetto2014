Prakriti



  Prakriti aims to provide an excellent opportunity for the young buzzing minds to compete with the best and brightest from all over the country and hone their techno-environmental skills and knowledge in the area of environmental science, technology and management.

   With multi-dimensional events like Paper Presentations and Case Studies, E-Quiz, Panel Discussion, workshop, the event will prove to be a great learning and networking opportunity for the participating students.

  It will be confluence of various stakeholders related to the field of environment science and engineering. It will bring together representatives of the industry, government, academicians, and non-governmental organizations to one platform and will endeavour to address their concerns and put forward innovative solutions to the burning problems in the environment field. The meet will also aim at capacity building by providing training programs in the relevant fields.
