E-Summit



The Entrepreneurship Summit 2014 is an initiative by E-Cell, ISM Dhanbad to bridge the gap between students mind and his dream to become an entrepreneur. E-SUMMIT will be a platform for learning & interaction of students, entrepreneurs, faculties, industry leaders etc, from across the World. It should be a mix of Key Note Speakers, motivational speech sessions, Events, Workshops, Start-Up Camp, Panel Discussion and Round Table Conference.
