QUIZZYPET

Introduction

A fantabulous opportunity for the students to get an insight into oil and gas industry through rounds of brainstorming quiz.

Abstract


Event structure:

1. Prelims (online):
Top 5 teams to be selected for the subsequent rounds.
2. Round 1: "Direct Hit"
Set of direct questions for each of the teams
3. Round 2:"Whats Your Pick?"
Teams get to choose from several displayed topics.
4. Round 3:"De Dana Dan"
A rapid fire round
5. Final Round: "Buzz on"

Buzzer round

Rules:

1. Online registration begins on October ,2013
2. Last date for registration: October,2013
3. Teams must register themselves online to make themselves eligible for the prelims.
4. Each team should consist of 3 members
5. Questions will be primarily from hydrocarbon sector
6. Decisions of the organizers will be final
