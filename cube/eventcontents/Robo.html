Abstract

DESCRIPTION



The event aims at executing the set of tasks in a limited amount of time. The objective of the event is to enhance the level of accuracy along with the speed of the participating bots. However things aren't as easy as they seem to be.
Surprise awaits you....

STRUCTURE OF THE GAME:

Round 1: The participants have to make a manually operated bot that can traverse in water and can fight with the competing bot in the specified arena.This round test the speed and strength of the bot.
Round 2: This round requires a bot that can traverse both on land and water. The bot have to perform a task of transporting the object from one island to another within a specified time limit and along a definite track.
Round 3: This round tests the accuracy of the participants. The finalists have to complete a maze without touching its boundaries. If the boundaries are touched a LED will glow and points of the team will be deducted.

SPECIFICATIONS

* Maximum bot size: 15cm x 20cm
* The bot should be manually operated on both land & water
* The bot must have a metallic strip attached to its sides
* Track width: 25cm
* Number of blocks to be displaced: 03
* Dimensions of the objects to be displaced: 05cm x 05cm

RULES AND REGULATIONS

* A team can consist of a maximum of 5 members.
* Inter college teams are allowed.
* The participating bot should be manually operated.
* The bots you build will first be checked for size and safety.
* The machines should not contain any combustible, corrosive or any other dangerous materials for safety reasons.
* Power supply will be provided.
* Judges have the right to disqualify any team if its bot has a mechanism that can be dangerous or harmful in any way.
* In case of any dispute the decision of the judges will be considered as final and binding to all, no arguments will be entertained.

JUDGEMENT CRITERION

* Round 1 will be a knockout between two teams. The team crossing the exit first or which will knockout the other bot will qualify for the second round.
* Round 2 will solely be based on time. The teams will be provided a maximum of 5 minutes. The team displacing more number of boxes will qualify for the final round. In case of a tie, the time taken will be considered as the qualifying criterion. Only top 5 teams will qualify for the final round.
* The final round evaluation will be based on the accuracy of the team. The time in which the team completes the maze will be noted. Penalty time of 5sec will be added every time the bot touches the boundaries of the maze. The team having minimum time will be declared as the winners.
