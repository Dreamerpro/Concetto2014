MIND FUZZY

Introduction

Tired of working with your hands? Want to exercise your brain a bit? Then mind fuzzy is just the right event for you.Two stages of intense puzzling and a lot of prize money is up for the grabs. quiz will cover a wide range of topics and will test your proficiency in logic, reasoning and mathematics. This time mind fuzzy concetto 2k14 comes with a different flavor.

Abstract

RULES :

Team of maximum of 3 members is allowed.

STAGE 1 :

Written exam in which teams would be given a set of questions.
A limited number of teams would be selected for the further round.

STAGE 2 :

Quiz among the selected teams.
Type of questions you can expect: Tricky and logical maths questions,
puzzles, brain teasers, trivia and technological questions.
