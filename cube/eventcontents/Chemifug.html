Introduction

CATALYCA invites participants to manifests their ideas on the topic & present it technically. It is the golden opportunity to remove stage fear and to improve soft skills.

Abstract

Team size is restricted to maximum of 2 members.

We encourage the originality, understanding, depth of content & comprehensiveness of the paper.

ROUND 1:

For the screening purpose, only abstract/brief summary of the paper of max. Of 2 pages must be mailed to "catalyca@rediffmail.com" with the subject : "PAPER PRESENTATION" along the name, email, contact no. of all the members.
The abstract should be a .doc/.pdf document attachment.
The short listed teams will be allowed to present their paper on the day of event.

ROUND 2:

# A presentation has to be created by the participants based on their paper which should be presented in the presence of the judges. The entire report/paper in form of three hard copies and one soft copy should be brought to the venue for judges.
# Each team will get a maximum of 12 minutes to articulate important features/points of their paper through PowerPoint Presentation in front of panel of judges and then 5 minutes for questions by the judges & or the students attending the presentation.

Topics:

1. Green Chemistry and Technology
2. Organic Solar Cells
3. Bio-fuels and Bioengineering
4. Shale Gas in India
5. Biological Sewage/ Wastewater Treatment
6. Enhanced Oil Recovery.
