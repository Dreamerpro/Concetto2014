Petro Panancea

Introduction

When we face any kind of problem, the solution always arises with analysis of the circumstances we are involved in. So here comes a chance to shake your grey matter and show off your creative and analysing skills! Petrogaia 2014 presents Petro Panacea, a productive and dynamic case study competition which provides the opportunity for teams coming from various colleges to learn from and compete against different participating colleges, in a domain empowering teamwork, harnessing competitiveness and enhancing innovative ideas. The main goal of this event is for students to display their innovative skills to analyse the real challenges faced in the Petroleum Industry and provide constructive solutions for them. The problem statement will be posted in the Concetto website.

Abstract


Event Structure:

1. Prelims : Abstract Selection (Online)
The abstracts and the corresponding teams selected advancing to the next round will be notified though emails.
2. Final Round:
The selected teams will present their final presentations and a common questionnaire round will be conducted in the end.

Format for Abstract:
* Submit the abstract in .doc or .pdf format.
* Title (Font Size-14, Bold, Upper Case)
* The abstracts should contain name of the participants (Font Size-12, Bold, Sentence Case) and their email ids.
* Name of the Institution (Font Size-12, Sentence Case)
* The abstract must be written in 500-750 words maximum.
Note: All fonts should be in Times New Roman
