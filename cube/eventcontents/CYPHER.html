CYPHER DECODING

Introduction

My event is an exquisitely crafted online challenge modeled on the lines of a treasure hunt. The event assesses the participants for their sharp wits, general knowledge, lateral thinking ability as well as the awareness about random happenings. A tad bit of common sense coupled with good observation, is what is takes you to enter the event that is bound to be an all-time favorite among students all over the country, like always.

Abstract

Rules and Regulations: The event will be online in which the participants will be asked to register and will be given login details. There will be set of questions which will be to an extent unique for individual participant. Every question will have points. The difficulty level of questions will increase as participant advances in the event. Every participant's progress will be reflected in scoreboard. Catch 1. Every question will have requisite number of hints. After each hint revealed, marks will be deducted from the 'Maximum score' of the question.
