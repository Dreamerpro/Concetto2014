Introduction

Does Electrical Engineering run in your blood?
Do you ever find yourself wondering how the internet is so amazing?
Does the working of electronic devices fascinate you?
Have you ever felt that if you were given a chance to go back in time and re-invent the cell phone, you could have made it better?
Have you ever been jealous, of all those stalwarts who invented the world we live in, at this moment in time?
If you answered yes to any of those questions, then this is the event for you! Concetto Simu-eleKtriKa will give you the perfect oppurtunity to showcase your talents and make some easy cash along the way. Work on some of the most cutting-edge technologies in the world of electronics and be amazed at what you are capable of.

Abstract

This can be an event where problem statements for 5 particular circuits will be given. Participants have to make the circuit in and simulate .The circuit will have more than one solution, the most efficient solution will have highest marks .Marks will be also awarded to the students for their ranks .The cumulative highest mark will be awarded the prize. Level of the questions will also increase gradually therefore markings for each question will also be different.
