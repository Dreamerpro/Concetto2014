General Rules


1. Any undergraduate or masters student of any discipline is eligible to participate.
2. Each team may have a maximum of four members.
3. This event will be conducted in two rounds.

Abstract Submissions: Last date 16 October

1) Abstract should not exceed 300 words and top of abstract must have Name(s), Name of college, Email address(s), contact number(s), Name of the department you wish to present the paper in, & Topic of paper presentation. Abstract should be in Std. IEEE two column format.
2) Participants have to send in the abstract of the research/ review work including the keywords, references, the idea proposed with figures (if needed) to student.asme@gmail.com till 20th March.
3) Your email should have the department name as your subject and your file name as the title of the concerned paper.
4) Selection to the next round for final presentations will be based on the abstract submissions.

Full Paper Submissions

The second round will be conducted at Indian School Of Mines, Dhanbad during Concetto 2014.
1. Participants should submit their full paper in ".doc" and ".pdf" format. You need to bring three hard copies of your complete paper (i.e. printed report) on the first day.
2. Formatting rules should be strictly followed in IEEE format. (One can download it from website).
3. Participants should ensure that their papers include all relevant figures, tables and bibliography.

Final Presentations

1. Final presentations should be in ".ppt" form at strictly and should be timed for 12 minutes (10 minutes for presentation and 2 minutes for Q&A round).
2. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
