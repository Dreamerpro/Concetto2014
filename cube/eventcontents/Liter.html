Abstract

1day worshop to teach how to make cheap solar powered bulbs.
Worshop is in association with Kartavya Bulbs made during this workshop would be donated to kartavya organization.
The bulb is nothing but a 1.5 liter clear PET bottle which is used as source of light. It is filled with the mixture of waterand bleach.This kit is embedded on the roofs of houses which act as a source of light.
