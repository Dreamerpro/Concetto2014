Introduction

Industries are no way apart from engineering college. They are the two faces of a single coin. This event will provide engineering students a chance to come up with their thoughts, ideas and the most important innovation in terms of development and efficiency of industries. Fresh brains have fresh idea and they need a platform to cook the idea. So, this is the most awaiting platform that the most innovative and creative brains needed.

Abstract


The event will involve two stages.

  1. In the first stage participating team has to design a model of an ideal refinery with the help of material provided in the kit at the time of event. Participants are supposed to design the model in such a manner which can mitigate the challenges faced by the refineries. Emission of harmful gases, waste water treatment, geographical location, sustainability, conservation of energy, sulfur content etc. are the major challenges involved.

  2. In the second stage participating team has to give a presentation to describe their model. All the characteristic features involved in the design incorporating the above mentioned challenges need to be explained in the presentation stage.

  Rules & Regulations:

1. Participating team will be of maximum four members.
2. Designing kits will be provided at the time of event (*free).
3. There will be a time limit to designing and presentation stage.
