Gizmo's Luck

Introduction

Have you ever imagined yourself locked up in a room and the only way out is by designing the key using an unknown software!!!
Here you are with the opportunity to test yourself for situations like this.
It is an on the spot event based on graphical software,which will be a surprise, with lots of brain exercise required which will be increased in subsequent rounds.The participants will be asked to make several 3D figures using the software by opting the best possible tool provided in that software.
All that is required is your imagination capabilities, sharpness of mind and decisison making potentials.

Abstract

Rounds:-

1) Directly shapes are given and you will have to implement them using the given software, within the time provided.
2) The shapes are not given directly but some clues are provided which are hidden in the riddles.
3) Now, here comes the final round, where the clues are given using 'C' language programs and also can be hidden in the environment you are working over.
So just crack the code and have the clue. Use the clue to get the shape.

Decision:- The winner at each round will be decided on the basis of number of design formed, neatness of the design and the efficient use of the software provided.
Number of teams selected will decided on the basis of the total paricipation.The decision of the panel will be final.

Venue: Computer Centre,CSE Department.
