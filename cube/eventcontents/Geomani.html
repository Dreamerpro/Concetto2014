Geomania

Introduction

GEOMANIA will be a quiz competition organised by Dept. of Applied Geology involving petro and geology related quiz testing the intellectual and application aspect of Petroleum engineering, Geology and Geophysics students as well as the basic understanding of the subject and your intelligence. So, Gear up to face the brain storming challenge for your knowledge of Earth Sciences, General Awareness & Logical Reasoning!
