Bizkraft

Introduction

An annual business plan competition of Indian School of Mines, Dhanbad which is the great Networking opportunity with Investors, Mentors, Incubators and other Ecosystem players. Direct entry into the MSME (Micro Small and Medium Enterprise) ISM Dhanbad Chapter and chance to get Incubation money of INR 6.5 lakhs from the side of MSME for the top three entries and a recommendation from our side too.

Abstract

Participants stand to benefit and gain in following manner:

a) Great Networking opportunity with Investors, Mentors, Incubators and other Ecosystem players.
b) Direct entry into the MSME (Micro Small and Medium Enterprise) ISM Dhanbad Chapter and chance to get Incubation money of INR 6.5 lakhs from the side of MSME for the top three entries and a recommendation from our side too.
c) Exciting Cash Prizes to be given to the top three winners.
d) To provide a meaningful learning experience for participants who are interested in pursuing an entrepreneurial opportunity at some point in their careers.
e) Webinars to increase your understanding of how to 'Make it Large' while starting a venture.

RULES & REGULATIONS



Eligibility criteria and team composition:

Eligibility:
* Students, Start-ups and Working Professionals from all over the world can participate.
* Entries must be the original work of entrants.
* Individual participation is allowed in the Competition though participation in teams is encouraged.
* The plan should solve a critical social problem in an innovative, transformational way.
* The mission and practices of the venture must focus on quantifiable social return.
* The venture must be for-profit and financially viable; non-profit entries will not be accepted.

Team Size: 4

* The event will be held in three stages.
* The maximum team size for all the rounds will be 4.
* On the basis of the performance in the first round, 10 teams will be selected for the final round.
* Entries submitted after the deadlines will not be considered.
* The judges, mentors and the organizers will guarantee the utmost confidence of the entry or idea.

Stage- 1:

* Executive summary of the plan has to be submitted.
* The submission will be online at Email id of Genesis.
* Rules and restrictions on the executive summary:
− The executive summary shall be submitted in a .pdf or .doc or .docx format only. No other form of submission will be accepted.
− The last date of submission of executive summary for the 1st round of this event is 10th October, 2014.
− Short listing of teams will be done on the basis of their executive summaries and innovation.
− Mail the abstract to genesis@ismu.ac.in
* 10 Teams to be short listed after Round - 1.

Stage 2:-

Mentoring Period – 11th October to 13th October, 2014.
Separate E-mentors will be provided to all the 10 finalists.
Adequate mentorship to all the 10 entries during this period from our associates.
Mentors for each team would be chosen by us.Stage 3-

Final Presentation-

* The 10 teams will give their presentation in front of the judges/mentors.
* The teams will have to give a presentation. Each team will get 12 minutes to present their B-plan. They will have 2 minutes to wind up.
* After the presentation there will be a 5 minute question answer session by the judges.
* 10% marks will be deducted for every minute exceeded after 15 minutes.
* Participants will have to give their presentation in Macromedia flash 8.8/Microsoft Power point.
* Picture clippings and graphs may be used.
* Recorded dialogues or movie clippings are not allowed.
* Laptops for presentation will be provided by us.
* Entries remain the property of the legal owner.
The contestants must agree with the rules of the contest and must agree to accept the declaration of confidentiality.
