Abstract

Four people together can form a single team.
Four programs will be given to a single team. Each team member has the freedom of choosing which question he or she would like to attempt first.
Then at the sound of a beep, the positions should be changed with the next partner of the team in clockwise direction.
The beep can be sent at any time as there is no fixed interval.
Criteria for winning
The team which codes the all four programs completely with the desired output first wins the competition. In case of a tie, the code with lesser number of lines shall be preferred.
Participation
Each team can contain exactly four members. Greater or less than four members will not be allowed to take part.
