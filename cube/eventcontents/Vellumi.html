Introduction

    Have you ever sat gazing at the marvels of the petroleum engineering spread across the pages of history and wondered, "How did they do it"? Then it's the time you can learn and show your talent pool through the Technical Paper Presentation.

    Vellumin encourages you to use all your engineering skills and bring to life a bridge, your own design, your own marvel!!. You can take part and enhance your knowledge about energy resources and could win prizes at the same time. Participants from different colleges will be competing. So you will get a great opportunity to compete against those participants and enhance your technical and presentation skills. Take your step and submit your abstract.
