A GAME OF CODES

Introduction

Have you ever prided yourself in coming up with the most bizarre solutions to simple problems? Do you enjoy experimenting with your code, toying with unusual semantics? Come this concetto 2k14 and experience all that and more at A Game of codes. If you think you are the best in the field of programming, then concetto 2k14 is the place for you. With questions varying from a range of topics to participants from all over the country.

Abstract


First stage--> online (time limit - 3hrs)

STAGE ONE

Round 1: (ON 14/10/14 9:00 PM to 12:00 PM ) {registration link: http://www.hackerrank.com/a-game-of-codes} Three question will be given let's say Q1, Q2, Q3 that too of the same difficulty level and participant will be free to do any one in the given time period.
Those who'll submit the correct answer will qualify for stage 2.


Round 2: (ON 16/10/14 9:00 PM to 12:00 PM ) The participants who qualified stage 1 with Q1 will be given set A
The participants who qualified stage 1 with Q2 will be given set B
The participants who qualified stage 1 with Q3 will be given set C.
Three questions will be provided in each of the sets. Select any one question from your set, solve the question in order to qualify for second round.

STAGE TWO -> offline (time limit- 2 hrs)
The participant who submits all the questions given in round 2 in least time will be the winner and similarly the runner up and second runner up will be decided.
ONLY SINGLE PLAYERS.

LANGUAGES:

Contestants can use any of the following languages:
C, C++, Java.
