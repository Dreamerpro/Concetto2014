Introduction

"Basic research is what I'm doing when I don't know what I'm doing." Poster presentation is the presentation of any scientific research or an idea in the form of a poster. The aim of this event is to test the technical depth of knowledge along with the creativity and the presentation skills of the participants. A good poster is one which is appealing to the viewer and which is understood easily. Distribution of handouts describing in brief the idea of your theme is a good way to get your poster remembered and gives a better understanding of the poster.

Abstract

Topics: 1 .Data Integration (Logs, well tests and Seismics)
2. Unconventional Resources: Potential for future
3. Geological evolution
4. Challenges in near-surface Geophysics
5. Identification and Mapping of Petroleum Systems
6. Exploration in Fractured basement plays
7. Geophysical data Processing
8. Advanced geophysical methods for reservoir characterization
