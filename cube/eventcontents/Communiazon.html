Introduction

10 Years back we lived in a technology which was in a primitive stage. Raw knowledge to make out what we ideate. Wireless technology too had a history which made it in a form we currently use. Now we have wireless tech like Bluetooth, Wifi, radio etc. which are available in modular form. So to really know how it works let's get back to the basics. From raw tools, design a device to wirelessly transmit your secret message!

Abstract

Quiz on wireless technology-

It is a knock out round. Teams will be asked a set of questions, each question will carry equal weightage. At the end of the round few teams will be shortlisted for the next round based on the basis of their score.
ROUND 2

In this round the teams will be given a problem statement and they will have to design the circuit and simulate the same on software like Orcad/Multisim etc.

ELIGIBLITY

> Maximum of 4 members are allowed per team.
> Individual participation is allowed.

JUDGING CRITERIA

> Decision of judges will be final.
