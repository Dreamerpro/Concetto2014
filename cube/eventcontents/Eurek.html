Eureka

Introduction

EUREKA is a case study that will give you an opportunity to get acquainted with problems in industry and suggest method to tackle them. Problem statement is provided and participants are required to prepare a solution in form of PowerPoint presentation.

Abstract



Eligibility:


The participant students must be currently pursuing education (undergraduate or postgraduate) in a recognized institute. The participants may be asked to furnish the supporting documents as to prove the fore mentioned criteria. In case of wrong information it leads to disqualification of team.



Event Structure:

1. The event consists of two phases i.e. preliminary round comprising of short listing and second round comprising of presentation which will held at ISM Dhanbad during CONCETTO'14.
2. In preliminary round participants has to submit the solution to the problem statement online before the completion of the deadline of the event.
3. Short listing of the teams would be on the basis of merit and short listed candidates will be eligible to attend the grueling session of CASE STUDY at ISM Dhanbad during CONCETTO'14.
4. Second round comprises of presentation cum questioning round where by team has to present their work on that problem statement.
5. Winners will be declared based on their performance in second round only. First round is just an eligibility criterion for second round.


Rules:

1. Only teams who have registered online would be able to participate.
2. Each team will contain maximum Three (3) participants. Students from different colleges can form a single team.
3. Teams should mention their college name, their ID, and the names of their members on the first page of the word file.
4. The submission has to be as a word document which should be mailed to concettofme14@gmail.com.
5. The document should not be longer than 6 pages including the abstract.
6. The whole analysis of the problem statement should be done by the participants themselves. Plagiarism will attract cancellation of participation.
7. Teams presenting their analysis in the second round should come with a proper presentation of not more than 20 slides along with their word document submitted in the first round.
8. Teams qualified for second round are required to bring 3 printed copies of their presentation and submit before the event starts.
9. The decision of the judges would be final and binding.
10. Only Top three teams will get certificate of merit. However other teams would get a certificate of participation.


Important Dates :
1. Deadline for Submission of Word Document for First round selections is 17th October,2014.
2. Shortlisted entries will be announced on 18th October, 2014.
