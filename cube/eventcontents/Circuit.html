Rules and Regulations:


* Each team can have a max of 2 members.
* This event will be conducted in 2 phases (prelims and Finals)
* Five teams will be selected after the prelims for circuit debugging.
* The finalists will be selected on time basis.
* Calculators are not allowed
* Decision of judges will be final

Prelims:

Preliminaries will be a written test with 15 questions (objective types & circuits with a error in it) duration of the test is 30 min.

Finals

This round will be of 10 questions which involves circuits of higher complexities. Time allotted for this will be 45 min.

Judging Criteria:

* In the absence of complete debugging, the partial debugging will also be considered and the prizes will be distributed accordingly.
* 1st & 2nd prize will be awarded.
