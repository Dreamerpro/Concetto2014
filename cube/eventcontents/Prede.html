Introduction

It is like a game including theoretical knowledge, practical use of electronic components and efficient use of resources.

Abstract


Stages:


Step 1- Short listing students on the basis of a written exam (theoretical).
Step 2- Giving them virtual money as per their scores in the exam.
Step 3- Giving them a task of designing a circuit for a problem given directly or through a video (for making it interesting).
Step 4- They will use their virtual money to buy the components required to design the network.
Step 5- Winner will be decided on the basis of time taken and money left with them.
