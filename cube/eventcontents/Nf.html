Racing inspires everyone. Every computer user has played a racing game sometime. This now evolves from a leisure habit to competing against best players. Come play and prove your skill in the Game of Need for Speed: Most Wanted. Winners will get cash prizes!

Abstract



Gameplay :


The game will take place from 17th to 19th October, at ISM.

DAY 1-
Two players will compete with each other. The winner will qualify for the next round which has to be held on day 2.The loser will be disqualified from the tournament. However he may again enter by re-registration on day 1 itself.

DAY 2
On day 2 there will be a complete knock out round .All the winners of first round will compete with each other. At the end of the day 8 players will qualify for the final round on day 3

DAY 3
On day 3 there will be the quarter final, semi-final and final of the competition. 8 players will play each other out of which 4 winners will qualify for semi-finals and eventually the 2 winners will qualify for final. Winner of the final will be declared the champion of tournament.
