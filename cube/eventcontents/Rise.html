Gameplay :


This Event will take place for three days (17TH TO 19TH OCTOBER).

DAY 1-
Two Teams will compete with each other.Each team consists three players. The winner team will qualify for the next round which has to be held on day 2.The loser team will be disqualified from the tournament. However he may again enter by re-registration on day 1 itself.

DAY 2-
On day 2 there will be a complete knock out round .All the winning teams of first round will compete with each other. At the end of the day, 4 teams will qualify for the final round on day 2.

DAY 3-
On day 3 there will be the semi-final and final of the competition. 4 teams will play each other out of which 2 winners will qualify for finals and eventually the winner team of the final will be declared the champion of tournament.

11.Disconnection:
   i. Disconnection: Any disconnection of the connection between match players due to System, Network, PC, and/or Power problems/issues.
   ii. Intentional Disconnection: any offending player will be charged with a loss by forfeit upon judgment by the referee.
   iii. If the disconnection is deemed to be unintentional by the referee, the match will be restarted.
   iv. If any player does not agree to a match restart, that player will lose by default.
12.Penalty for Unfair play:
   i. Intentional disconnection.
   ii.Upon discovery of any player committing any violations regarded as unfair play, that player will be disqualified from the tournament.
   iii.During the course of any match, the operations staff and/or referee may determine other actions to be unfair play at any time.
   iv.Two warnings constitute being disqualified from the tournament.
