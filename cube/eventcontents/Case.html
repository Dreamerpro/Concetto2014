Case study-Centralized sewage Treatment Plant

Introduction

Centralized sewage treatment plant in ISM Dhanbad. Dhanbad city doesn't have a sewage treatment plant and neither ISM has one. This case study is just an initiative for the establishment of plant in ISM.The objective of the case study is to explore the talents of students in solving environmental problems of the industry or an organization.

Abstract



Topic: 1.
Inland Oil Spills: Environmental Issues and Clean-up.

2. The Ganges:
Pollution Sources and Treatment.

3. Kudremukh Mines:
Story of closure and reopening.
