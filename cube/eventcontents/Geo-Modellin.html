Geo-Modelling

Introduction

Geo-Modelling is basically a depiction of the inherent processes taking place In the Earth surface as well as in the sub surface. It also essentially includes the processes and the means of various geological activities like seismic data acquisition, exploration & exploitation process and mechanisms of the subsurface methods etc. Generally, it gives a broader perspective and the different aspects of their geological applications.

Abstract

Problem Statement:


Participating team need to make and present a model which can be working or static. Model should be innovative and should present a theme based on any Feature or phenomenon related to Geology, Geophysics and Petroleum engineering. Participants can present 3D design of model on any software.

Rules and Eligibility:



Following are the criteria for the participation in the Geo-modelling contest:

1. No. of participants in a team must not be more than 3 members.
2. Participants must be students of an undergraduate/ Integrated or a post graduate course
3. The prepared model should be of a geological/geophysical/petroleum setting or phenomenon and the same must be explained by the participants at the time of the competition.
