Introduction

The event encourages participants to know about wireless technology and to design a wirelessly handled module with the help of given module.

Abstract

Description of the event
The event is based on day to day use of wireless technology The event consists of 2 rounds.

Round 1
Quiz on wireless technology
Round 2
Using all given apparatus make a wirelessly handled module and transmit given signal.

ELIGIBLITY

> All students with a valid identity card of their respective educational institutes are eligible to participate.
> The team can consist of 4 members at maximum.
> Individual participation is allowed.


JUDGING CRITERIA

> User friendliness of the gadget.
> Time taken and range of the module.
> Decision of judges shall be final.
