Soccer Pursuit

So the game is all about showing your mentalality and skills . Come up guys.

Abstract

Objectives – Completion of challenge gaining the maximum points within minimum time.

The Game –
In the first round, Team of two bots will play soccer against each other on 1st floor dragging balls on other’s court carrying different points. After 4 minutes , a race shall be conducted on the ground floor having 2 laps with each bot completing a single lap. At the end of the race, the team comprising the maximum poins will qualify for the 2nd round.
For the 2nd round, there will be two floors both having one team member.On the first floor, member will have to drag blocks and the second bot will drag them to the initial point in the ground floor . Team collecting max.blocks will qualify for 3rd round.
In the third round,there will be a 2 lap race having hurdles and the winner shall be decided on the basis of time and points collected.

RULES –

1) In the first and second round, two bots shall constitute a single team. However, in the 3 rd round, a single bot shall constitute a team.
2)For the first round,20 points will be awarded for each perfect drop .Fighting is allowed in the football arena.
3) In the racing arena, team completing first shall be awarded 70 and next team shall get 40 points. A deduction of 10 points will be awarded every time the bot fells from the arena.
4) In the second round, 20 points shall be awarded for the bot dragging the block in the first floor and 30 points shall be awarded for the bot dragging the block on the ground floor in the specified area.
5) In the third round , individual bots in the racing arena completing the race in minimum time shall get maximum points.
6)However,a time period of 3 seconds per fall shall be added to the total time elapsed.
7)Cutting opponent team robo wire is not allowed.
