Brainquest

Introduction

If you are an enthusiastic puzzleteir and a good team player, cravingly waiting for an opportunity to showcase your skills, BRAINQUEST provides you the chance to showcase them by competing in a fierce battle of wits against the most intellectual of brains. With a variety of events from treasure hunts to presentations, BRAINQUEST provides you a quizzing experience you've never imagined.

Abstract


The event consists of 3 rounds-

i) CROSSING THE HURDLES-

It is the qualifying round of the event. Each participating team will be given a set of questions whose answers will have to be found through given set of clues in a limited period of time. The teams that clear this round will go ahead to the next round.

ii) GRAB THE IDEA-

The second and the most exciting round which will test the grasping and perception skills of the participants. In this round a video clip will be shown to each team carrying a hidden message or issue which the participants have to spot out. Only those teams who would grab the right idea of the video will proceed to the next stage.

iii) SHOWCASE-

The final round of the event will test the presentation skills of the participants. The teams will have to give a presentation on the topic grabbed from the video round.

iv) Each team may consist of maximum 4 members.
