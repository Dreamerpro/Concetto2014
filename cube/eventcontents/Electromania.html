Abstract


The contestants can give their presentation on the following topics :

* Anti-Reflective Nano-structures for High Efficiency Optical Devices
* Wireless Communication
* VLSI
* Embedded Systems
* High Speed Electronics Devices
* Photonic and Opto-Electronic circuits
* Biometrics & Bioinformatics
* Microcontroller and its applications
* Sensors and actuator systems
* Micro and molecular electronics
* RFID and Applications
* DSP and its applications
* Digital Image Processing
* Artificial Neural Networks
* Power system protection and control
* Recent Trends in Power Electronics & Drivers
* Non Conventional Energy Sources
* Mobile Adhoc Network
* WI-FI / WI-MAX
* Image processing and applications
* Image processing in computer vision
* Cyber Crime and Security


Other topics related to electrical & electronics the above mentioned fields will also be considered.

The rules of the contest are as follows :

1. The abstract must be very brief, and should not exceed 250 words.
2. The abstract alone must be sent before 15th Oct to concettoenvision@gmail.com.
3. Selected teams will be notified via email on or before 16th Oct
4. Once you have received an email confirming that your paper has been selected, you must send in your complete paper in IEEE format on or 16th Oct.
5. All documents that you send must be MS Word documents or PDF files only.
6. A paper can have two authors at most.
7. Participants will be allowed 10 minutes to present their papers (7 minutes for presenting the paper, 3 minutes for Q/A session).
8. Teams can send in multiple papers, but only one will be selected.
9. The decision of the judges shall be treated as final.
