Turbo-War

Introduction



What is it?

* It is an automotive quiz with sole aim to promote awareness related to automotive industry among young enthusiasts.

* When will it take place?

* It will take place on first and third day of annual techno-management fest, Concetto, of ISM Dhanbad.

Abstract


Basic Rules



Each team will comprise of two members.

* The quiz will have interactive sessions along with a written test.
* There would be direct and indirect questions on general and technical aspects of automotive industry.
* The number of teams selected for final round will be at discretion of the organizers.
* Any faulty approach may lead to disqualification


Fiery Fiesta (1st Round)


The first round will have a written test and 20 questions will be asked.

* This round will form basis of selection of teams in 2nd round.
* The questions will comprise general and technical aspects of automotive industry.
* It will also include questions related to the latest advancement and updates made in this industry over the years.


Ign-E-Gist(2nd Round)

Entrants will be selected on basis of marks scored by their team.

* This round will consist of direct and indirect questions related to a particular quiz pattern which will be revealed at the time of rounds.
* This round will also consist of audio-visual rounds.
* This round will be held on third day.


The Final Lap(Finale)

The student who have qualified the third round will compete amongst each other.

* The question pattern and other relevant data will be revealed at the time of quiz. Although, the question pattern will be similar.
* This round will also consist of audio-visual rounds.

Prizes

Prizes worth Rs. 3000/-
