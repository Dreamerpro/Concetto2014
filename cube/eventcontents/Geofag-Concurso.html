Geofag-Concurso 2.0

Introduction

Geofag-Concurso is an Earth Science and Space Quiz competition involving questions from all sectors of Earth Science, Galaxies as well as the dynamics of the Solar System. The quiz will test your basic knowledge on how much you know about your planet and the and there are mind boggling rounds that will force you to data mine your cerebrum.
So be ready to gain and unveil your knowledge of Earth sciences..!

Abstract


Students from any branch can participate.

Rules:

1. The team should consist a maximum of three members.
2. The final teams to compete will be selected by a preliminary round to be held on the day of the event.
3. There are exciting rounds having their own set of rules that will be announced on the Spot.
4. Use of any electronic devices such as mobiles, calculators, etc. will immediately lead to Dis-qualification.
5. The decision of quiz master shall be final and binding
6. There will be free on spot REGISTRATION on the same day starting from 10 am.
